Booking Process

The cruise booking process involves searching for available cruises based on your preferences. Once you make the payment, you'll receive a booking confirmation with all the details. Before the cruise, you'll complete pre-cruise planning tasks and collect necessary documents for embarkation.

Cancellation and Refund Policies

We offer full refunds (minus a cancellation fee) if you cancel 15 days before your cruise. Cancelling between 30 and 40 days prior gets you 50% back. Cancellations within 5 days of departure aren't refundable. No-shows and non-refundable bookings won't receive refunds. Changes to dates or plans might have fees, especially within 10 days of departure. Unforeseen events might lead to itinerary changes. For help, contact us at Info@cruisemate.co.za. Remember, policies vary, so check terms when booking.

Travel Documentation

Travel requires key documents: a valid passport, possibly a visa, and travel insurance for protection. Check entry needs, keep booking confirmations, and have digital/physical copies of all essential papers. Preparedness ensures a worry-free journey.
